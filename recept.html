🍞 Paska Recipe 🍞

🌟 Ingredients

  • 🍋 Zest of 1 lemon
  • 🌿 Cloves, cinnamon, cardamom (to taste)
  • 🍦 Vanilla
  • 🍬 Candied fruit – 50 g
  • 🌰 Almonds – 50 g
  • 🍇 Raisins – 50 g
  • 🌾 Flour – 650 g
  • 🧈 Butter – 200 g (softened)
  • 🥚 Egg yolks – 5
  • 🍭 Sugar – 150 g
  • 🧫 Yeast – 10 g
  • 🥛 Milk – 250 ml

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. 🌡️ Warm the milk and add the yeast and 200 g of flour. Let it rest in a warm place for 30 minutes.
  2. 🥣 In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks with the spices, vanilla, lemon zest, and sugar. Then add the softened butter and mix well.
  3. 🍞 Combine this mixture with the yeast mixture, add the remaining flour, and knead the dough. Cover and let it rise until doubled in size.
  4. 🥜 Once the dough has risen, mix in the chopped almonds, raisins, and candied fruit.
  5. 🧁 Transfer the dough into greased baking molds, filling them to one-third of their height. Let the dough rise again.
  6. 🔥 Once well risen, bake in a preheated oven at 170–180°C (340–355°F) until golden brown.
